ALCHEMY & IMAGINATION

HISTORY WITH A TWIST

The name and design take inspiration from the 19th-century quack doctors who embraced experimental chemistry and miraculous “cures.” One such figure, James Morrison, opened the British College of Health just a few doors from us. His fascination with botanical compounds and esoteric remedies sparked the playful spirit that runs through Hokus Pokus today.
